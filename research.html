What are formation environments of young stars and brown dwarfs like?

As part of the Taurus Boundary of Stellar/Substellar (TBOSS) Survey, I have used the Atacama Large Millimeter/Submillimeter Array (ALMA) to measure the dust content of protoplanetary disks around the lowest-mass stars and brown dwarfs in the Taurus star-forming region.
